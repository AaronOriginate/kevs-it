Before you get in touch

Answers to the questions we hear most from prospective clients.

How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 2 business hours. For urgent IT issues, our managed clients receive priority support with critical issues addressed within 1 hour.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Bristol and serve businesses across the UK. Our remote support capabilities mean we can assist clients nationwide, and we offer on-site support within our core service area in the South West and beyond.

Do you offer emergency out-of-hours support?

Yes — our premium managed IT clients receive 24/7 emergency support. For all other clients, we offer emergency call-out services at competitive rates. Contact us for details.

Is there a minimum contract length?

We offer flexible arrangements to suit your business. Our managed IT services typically start with a 12-month agreement, but we also provide ad-hoc support, one-off projects, and pay-as-you-go options.

Can I get a free IT health check?

Absolutely. We offer a complimentary IT health check for prospective clients. We'll assess your current infrastructure, identify risks, and provide actionable recommendations — no obligation attached.

What information should I prepare before getting in touch?

It helps to know the approximate size of your team, your current IT setup (if known), and what challenges you're facing. But don't worry if you're not sure — that's what we're here to help figure out.
