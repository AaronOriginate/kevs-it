[ The Founder ]

From UWE Bristol to Award-Winning IT

Kevin Rice founded Kevs IT with a simple belief: businesses deserve IT support that actually works. After graduating from UWE Bristol, Kevin spent years in the trenches — fixing servers at midnight, talking clients through crises, and learning exactly what businesses need from their technology partner.

That hands-on experience shaped everything Kevs IT stands for today. No hiding behind ticketing systems. No jargon-heavy reports that nobody reads. Just straight-talking IT support from people who genuinely care about getting it right.

Over 14 years later, Kevs IT has grown from a one-person operation to an award-winning team serving hundreds of businesses across the UK — but the philosophy hasn't changed. Every client gets the same dedication that Kevin put into his very first call-out.
